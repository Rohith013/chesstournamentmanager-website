Support Center

How can we help you?

Contact Support

If you have encountered a bug, have a feature request, or need assistance, please reach out to us. We are happy to help.

Email: gamewingssupport@gmail.com

Expected response time: within 3–5 business days.

Release Information

Latest Version: 1.0.0

Known Issues

Currently, there are no known major issues. If you find a bug, please email us with steps to reproduce it.

Release Notes (v1.0.0)

  • Initial release on the Google Play Store.
  • Support for Swiss System pairings.
  • Support for Round Robin scheduling.
  • Support for Knockout brackets.
  • Local player database management.
  • PDF generation for pairings, standings, and reports.
  • Complete offline functionality.
  • Dark mode optimized UI.

Bug Reports & Feature Requests

When reporting a bug, please include the following information in your email to help us resolve it quickly:

  • Your device model (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S23)
  • Your Android version (e.g., Android 14)
  • The app version (currently 1.0.0)
  • A description of what you were trying to do when the issue occurred
  • Steps to reproduce the issue

We also welcome feature requests! If there is a specific pairing rule, tie-break system, or export format you'd like to see, let us know.
