Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require internet to work?
No. Chess Tournament Manager is designed as an "offline-first" application. You can create tournaments, add players, generate pairings, and calculate standings entirely without an internet connection. Internet is only required if you choose to share or export files via email/messaging apps.
Can I export tournament reports?
Yes. You can export pairings, standings, and final tournament results as professional PDF files for printing or sharing.
What tournament formats are supported?
The application fully supports Swiss System, Round Robin (All-Play-All), and Knockout (Elimination) tournament formats.
Can I back up my tournaments and player data?
Yes. You can securely back up all your data to a local file on your device and restore it later or transfer it to another device.
