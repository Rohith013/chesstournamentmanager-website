Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Game Wings Support, we prioritize the privacy and security of your data. The Chess Tournament Manager application is designed with a strong focus on offline functionality and local data ownership.

1. Data Collection & Storage

  • No User Account Required: You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use the application.
  • Local Storage: All tournament data, player profiles, pairings, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Synchronization: In Version 1.0.0, there is no automatic cloud synchronization or backup to remote servers. Your data stays entirely on your device.
  • Exported Files: Any PDF reports or backup files you generate remain strictly under your control. You choose when and how to share them.

2. Analytics and Tracking

  • No Analytics: Version 1.0.0 does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or usage tracking mechanisms.

3. Advertisements

  • Ad-Free Experience: Version 1.0.0 does not display any advertisements.
  • Future Advertisements Notice: Future updates to the application may introduce optional premium features or advertisements. If this occurs, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly to reflect any changes in data handling or third-party integrations, while continuing to prioritize your privacy.

4. App Permissions

The application may request the following permissions on your device solely for core functionality:

  • Storage/Files: Required only to save PDF reports and backup files to your device's local storage.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect user data or transmit information over the internet, it is safe for users of all ages.

6. Contact Information
